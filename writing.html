Writing Guides

  • Writing in General:
    • If you share my level of obsessive-compulsive disorder, buy the Collins Cobuild English Guides (Vol. 1-10) (JD link for the Chinese edition)
    • The Elements of Style — the (probably) most famous American English writing style guide by William Strunk Jr. and E. B. White
    • Read this article before you hold up the above guide Bible-ishly
    • Ludwig Guru — a great linguistic search engine
  • For Mathematical Writing:
    • On Writing by Terence Tao
    • Mathematical Writing by Donald E. Knuth, Tracy Larrabee, and Paul M. Roberts
    • Whenever you are unsure about mathematical definitions/theorems/proofs/formalism, just refer to Elements of Mathematics by the Bourbaki group. (Yes, I'm a Bourbakist.)
  • If you are a heavy user of LaTeX:
    • Watch this lecture by Prof. Ryan O'Donnell for many useful tips that even an experienced LaTeX-er may not know
    • FiXme: the best package for annotation, especially when there are multiple collaborators
    • cleveref: a package for better cross-referencing
    • tcolorbox: a package for fancy boxes that are fully customizable
