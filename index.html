Xiao Liang （梁宵）

I'm an assistant professor in the CSE Department at CUHK. I'm interested in Cryptography and its intersections with related fields such as Quantum Computing, Computational Complexity, and Computer Security. My work has concentrated on theoretical fundamentals, including Zero-Knowledge Protocols, Secure Multi-Party Computation, Non-Malleability, Digital Signatures, as well as their practical applications.

I'm lucky to work with a group of curious and creative students. Together, we form the CryptoPlus Lab!

Prior to my appointment at CUHK, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at NTT Research, working with Vipul Goyal. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at Rice University, working with Nai-Hui Chia and Kai-Min Chung. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stony Brook University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Omkant Pandey.
