Xiao Liang    [Curriculum Vitae]

I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University. My advisor is Professor Omkant Pandey. Before joining the Ph.D. track, I received an M.S. degree in Applied Math from Stony Brook University and a B.S. in Economics from Beijing Institute of Technology.

I am interested in Cryptography and its interplay with Computational Complexity Theory. I worked on problems related to Zero-Knowledge Proofs, Secure Multi-Party Computation, and Non-Malleability. Meanwhile, I keep an open mind toward all the topics in Cryptography, being always happy to learn new things.

Theoretical Toys—my always-unfinished notes on mathematical tools for theoretical computer science.
